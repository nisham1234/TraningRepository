Local body polls 2025: members take oath in local bodies across Kerala, spotlight on Thiruvananthapuram Corporation

Panchayats, municipalities and Corporations across Kerala saw newly-elected members being sworn in on Sunday (December 21, 2025), even as the spotlight was on the Thiruvananthapuram Corporation where the Bharathiya Janata Party (BJP), with 50 seats, had emerged the single largest party in a stunning upset in the 2025 local body elections earlier this month.

Film fraternity bids farewell to actor Sreenivasan

Actor-director Sreenivasan’s residence at Kandanad near Kochi saw a steady stream of visitors since Sunday (December 21, 2025) morning as members of the film fraternity and fans crowded together to pay their last respects to him. Personalities from the film industry and political circles, along with Sreenivasan’s contemporaries, stood firm with the family as they tried to grapple with the irreparable loss. Early in the morning, actor Suriya arrived to pay his respects. “I have been a big fan of Sreenivasan sir and have closely followed his work. I heard the news while I was in Kochi, and it was deeply saddening. I wanted to come and see him. His writings and body of work will remain etched in everyone’s hearts. May his soul rest in peace,” he said Members of the film fraternity who visited included Parvathy Thiruvothu, Renji Panicker, Sathyan Anthikkad, Jagadish, and Prithviraj, among others. The overwhelming outpouring of grief was a testament to Sreenivasan’s stature. Through his sharp wit, humour, incisive political commentary, and unforgettable characters, he carved a unique niche for himself and left an indelible mark on the Malayalam film industry.
